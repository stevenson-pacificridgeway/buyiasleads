FAQ

Common questions about BuyIASLeads

When do I receive my leads? +

After your payment, you'll receive a confirmation email and our team will get your CRM access set up. Fresh prospects are then delivered to your dashboard as they convert, so you can start reaching out right away.

What if leads don't convert? +

All leads come pre-qualified and actively seeking indexed annuity solutions. We deliver the prospects—the conversion depends on your outreach and expertise. You have all the tools: CRM access, Annuity University link, Oscar Annuity AI, and a 52-week nurture campaign to support your follow-up.

How does billing work — one-time or monthly? +

Your leads are a one-time purchase. Your CRM seat is $100/month, charged with your first order and then automatically each month. You can cancel the CRM seat anytime to stop future charges — cancel before your next billing date to avoid renewal.

What's included with the CRM seat? +

Your $100/month CRM seat includes: the CRM dashboard, Annuity University link for training, Oscar Annuity AI for sales support, a free book on indexed annuities, and a 52-week automated email nurture campaign to keep leads engaged.

How are leads delivered? +

Leads are delivered directly into your CRM dashboard in real-time. As prospects convert and submit their information, they appear immediately in your account so you can reach out with zero delay.

What payment methods do you accept? +

We accept all major credit and debit cards (Visa, Mastercard, American Express, Discover) processed securely through Stripe. Your payment is encrypted and protected.

Do you offer discounts for larger packages? +

Our pricing is straightforward: 100 leads for $2,000, 200 leads for $3,000, or 400 leads for $4,000. Choose the package that fits your capacity and budget. All include full CRM access and tools.
