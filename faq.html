FAQ

Common questions about BuyIASLeads

When do I receive my leads? +

After your payment you'll get a confirmation email and we'll set up your CRM access. Your first leads typically arrive within a few business days, then continue flowing into your dashboard daily until your full order is delivered. Because each lead is freshly generated for you, a package fills over a period of days rather than all at once.

What if leads don't convert? +

All leads come pre-qualified and actively seeking indexed annuity solutions. We deliver the prospects—the conversion depends on your outreach and expertise. You have all the tools: CRM access, Annuity University link, Oscar Annuity AI, and a 52-week nurture campaign to support your follow-up.

How does billing work — one-time or monthly? +

Your leads are a one-time purchase. Your CRM seat is $100/month, charged with your first order and then automatically each month. You can cancel the CRM seat anytime to stop future charges — cancel before your next billing date to avoid renewal.

What's included with the CRM seat? +

Your $100/month CRM seat includes: the CRM dashboard, Annuity University for prospect education, Oscar (your 24/7 Annuity AI) for sales support, a free book on indexed annuities as a lead magnet, and a 52-week automated nurture (email and text) to keep prospects engaged between your calls.

How are leads delivered? +

Leads are delivered directly into your CRM dashboard. As each prospect opts in, they're added to your account and introduced to you by name, so you can reach out while they're warm.

Are the leads exclusive to me? +

Yes. Every lead you buy is generated for you and only you — never shared with other agents, never resold, and never recycled from an aged list. You're not racing five other people to the same prospect.

Which states can I get leads in? +

We generate leads in the states you're licensed in. Let us know your licensed states during onboarding and we'll target accordingly. You are responsible for holding the proper licenses and for complying with all applicable laws when contacting prospects.

What's your lead quality and replacement policy? +

Every lead is a real person who opted in expressing interest in an indexed annuity. If a lead's contact information is undeliverable or clearly invalid, contact us within 7 days and we'll review it in good faith and replace it where appropriate. We can't guarantee that any given prospect will answer, qualify, or buy — that depends on your outreach, licensing, and follow-through.

Can I get a refund? +

Because leads are a perishable, individually generated product, lead purchases are final once placed. If you believe leads were not delivered or were materially defective, reach out within 7 days and we'll review it. You can cancel the $100/month CRM seat anytime to stop future charges; fees already billed are non-refundable. See our Terms for details.

What is the 50-agent FIA partnership? +

Beyond one-off lead orders, we partner with a small group of agency owners who want the full done-for-you system — exclusive FIA leads, the nurture engine, and the playbook. We keep the group small so we can support it properly. See the FIA partnership page or book a call.

Who is behind BuyIASLeads? +

BuyIASLeads was built by Gregory Stevenson, author of Indexed Annuity Secrets, who generates and closes these leads in his own agency. It's operated by Pacific Ridgeway Insurance Solutions; charges may appear on your statement as “HORIZON BUYIASLEADS.”

What payment methods do you accept? +

We accept all major credit and debit cards (Visa, Mastercard, American Express, Discover) processed securely through Stripe. Your payment is encrypted and protected.

Do you offer discounts for larger packages? +

Our pricing is straightforward: 100 leads for $2,000, 200 leads for $3,000, or 400 leads for $5,000. Choose the package that fits your capacity and budget. All include the $100/month CRM seat and tools.
